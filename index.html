О нас

OUR STORY

the start-up Neobis was founded in 2016 by two girls, AUCA student programmers, Sanira and Begimai.

OUR IDEOLOGY

improving the quality of IT infrastructure in Kyrgyzstan through increasing the professionalism and preparedness of graduate programmers

OUR MISSION

gratuitous mentoring of students-programmers to improve practical skills, subsequent employment of club programmers in large IT companies in Kyrgyzstan

WE BELIEVE

in learning through the development of commercial projects. This is effective for understanding the theoretical basis taught in universities.

WE HELP

businesses in a short time and a small budget to get software to improve their services

WE SOLVE

low level of preparedness of the programmer intern. (The problem of not only one person, but the whole country)

HackFest: Hackathon

The mobile application is developed for the Central Asian hackathon within the framework of HackFest "Generation Z: well-being of children" which took place on March 23-25, 2018. Organized by the Central Asian Coalition on promotion of the rights of children and women, PF "League of Advocates for the Rights of the Child", IT organization Neobis in conjunction with The United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF), the Embassy of the Netherlands, the Information Agency AKIpress, the American University of Central Asia.

The main goal of this event is to develop innovative solutions based on digital technologies, aimed at improving the quality of life and eradicating violence against children in Central Asia.

The mobile application is informational in nature, which contains information about hakathon, ads, a list of partner companies, a list of participants and mentors and much more.

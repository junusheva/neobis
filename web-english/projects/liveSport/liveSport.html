LiveSport

LiveSport - sports platform of Kyrgyzstan. With this application you will always be aware of the events of all sports news in Kyrgyzstan. Also you can leave your ads or view others and find out what sports events will be held in the near future.            Moreover, you can keep track of different promotions for gyms, courses and other sports offers. LiveSport gives you the opportunity to meet and chat with people with the same sporting interests in the Chat section. Here you can create a group with sports themes and start a conversation. You have the opportunity to broadcast live events in live broadcast.
